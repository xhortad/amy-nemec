ABOUT

I am a designer that started out doing graphic and web design. It didn’t take long for me to spark an interest in bringing my web designs to life, so I began to migrate to front end web development. Recently, I have become excited with having the opportunity to take my designs and make sure they are not just “pretty” but are focused on the users experience and the usability. Wire framing and rapid prototypes help shape the way a project is driven before it gets to development and that is where all the fun begins!

Here are a few highlights:

  • A creative, goal oriented UI/UX Designer with experience creating responsive designs
  • Self-motivated team player that enjoys working in a fast paced environment with excellent multitasking and prioritization skills
  • Been awarded numerous internal corporate awards for going above and beyond my everyday responsibilities
  • I am eager to continue to grow keeping up on current technologies and helping you get your business to the next level
